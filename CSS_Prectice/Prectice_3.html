Electronic Commerce

Information Technology has transformed the way people work. Electronic Commnerce has unleashed yet another revolution,which is changing the way businesses buy and sell products and services.

E-Commerce Types

A businesses organization can organise itself to conduct e-commerce with its trading partners, which are businesses and / or with customer.The resulting modes of doing bussiness are referred to as Business-to-Business(B2B) e-commerce and Business-to-Consumer(B2C). There is another category of e-commerce,know as Consumer-to-Consumer(C2C). The auction site run by business organisation falls under this category.
